D3 Chart Visualizing Mike Tyson's Knockout Times

What is this?

This chart project uses the d3.js library and visualizes the professional career knockouts of retired boxer ‘Iron’ Mike Tyson. The individual bars on the chart represent matches, and the length of each bar represents the length of time from when the fight started until the time of the fight stoppage.

Click here to see the chart

Features

On desktop, tooltips show additional details of the match (type of knockout,opponent’s name, date of match, round and round time of stoppage). On mobile, the text content is sized for screen width, however the chart itself is not (the user can still zoom and pan to see the full chart, this was a design compromise).

Why did I make this?

I wanted to learn D3 js, and wanted a subject that would keep me interested while learning it. I have enjoyed Mike Tyson’s presence in the media and was a fan of the Nintendo Entertainment System video game that he appeared in back in the 80s (Mike Tyson’s Punch-Out!!).
